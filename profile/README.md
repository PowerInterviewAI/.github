## Power Interview AI

**Privacy-first AI interview assistant** for technical and behavioral interviews-built for real, live interview scenarios.

**Power Interview AI** helps job seekers perform better with **real-time transcription**, **context-aware reply suggestions**, **coding assistance**, and **smart transcript exports**-while keeping privacy at the core. Sensitive data is designed to stay **on your device** with secure local storage and user-controlled information.

### What we’re building
- **Live Transcription (Dual-Channel):** Speaker-aware transcription with full conversation history  
- **AI Reply Suggestions:** Personalized, context-aware responses based on your **CV**, **job description**, and the **ongoing conversation**  
- **Code Suggestions:** Screenshot-based coding help with syntax-highlighted solutions  
- **Stealth Mode:** Discreet on-screen operation (hotkeys, opacity, positioning) designed to be **non-capturable** during screen share  
- **Smart Export:** DOCX transcript export with AI summaries, insights, and communication-pattern analysis
